Step 1 – Area Calculation

Area = Length × Width

Step 2 – Total Lumens Required

Total Lumens = Lux × Area

Step 3 – Number of Fixtures

Fixtures = Total Lumens / (Lumens per Fixture × UF × MF)

Step 4 – Spacing (SHR Method)

Max Spacing = Mounting Height × SHR (1.5)

Step 5 – Layout Arrangement

Columns = L / Max Spacing Rows = Total Fixtures / Columns

Step 6 – Power Density

Power Density = Total Power / Area (Assuming 100 lm/W LED efficiency)
