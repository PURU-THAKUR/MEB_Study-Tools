Step 1: Secondary Fault Current

Isec = Ifault / CT Ratio

Isec = A

Step 2: Relay Burden Resistance

Rrelay = VA / (5²)

Rrelay = Ω

Step 3: Total Secondary Resistance

Rtotal = Rrelay + Rlead

Rtotal = Ω

Step 4: Required Voltage

Vrequired = Isec × Rtotal × 2

Vrequired = V

Step 5: CT Capability Voltage

Vct = ALF × 5A × Rtotal
